Gen Z Terms

Simp

Meaning: A person who is overly attentive or submissive to someone they’re interested in.

Usage: "Stop simping for her, dude."

Origin: Started as "simpleton," meaning a foolish person. It later evolved to describe men who were overly submissive to women. Today, it's used to mock men who go overboard for someone they like, often losing self-respect.



Delulu

Meaning:Short for "delusional", often used humourously.

Usage: "if i manifest hard enough, he'll fall in love with me. I'm entering my delulu era."

Origin: First popularized in K-pop stans -- it's been adopted across Gen Z culture to jokingly refer to wishful, over-the-top or out-of-touch thinking


Slay

Meaning: To succeed at something with style or confidence.

Usage: "You slayed that runaway, girl!."

Origin: Originally popularized in Black and Latinx LGBTQ+ ballroom culture, especially in the New York drag scene. It was used to describe fierce performance, fashion or attitude.


Ghosted

Meaning: When someone cuts off all communication without explanation.

Usage: "We were texting everyday and then she ghosted me."

Origin: The term ghosting first started appearing in online dating culture in the early 2010s. The word entered mainstream media around 2015 when news outlets began covering modern dating behavior


Bet

Meaning: Agreement, approval, or saying "okay"

Usage: "You coming to the party?"----"Bet"

Origin: Originates from African American Vernacular english(AAVE) and has evolved overtime within black communities, particularly in urban centers and hip-hop culture.

Gen Z Emojis

🫣

Meaning: Used when you're shocked, curious, or lowkey embarrassed.

Usage: "When I saw the drama on TikTok 🫣."

💀

Meaning: Represents laughing so hard you're "dead."

Usage: "That meme is so funny I’m dead 💀."

😭

Meaning: Over-exaggerated Laughter, Relatable sadness or drama.

Usage: "That joke was so dumb 😭."

🗿

Meaning: used sarcastically or seriously to call someone a legend.

Usage: "Bro rejected free pizza because he’s on a diet 🗿"

🥶

Meaning: Something/someone is "cold"= savage or cool

Usage: "Joanna roasted me in front of everyone🥶.

🤡

Meaning: "I'm dumb" or "they're dumb."

Usage: "Vedant doesn't know to speak telugu 🤡".

👀

Meaning: Watching, waiting or tea(gossip)

Usage: "Vedant liked her post again 👀".

🔥

Meaning: Cool, attractive or impressive.

Usage: "His dance was awesome🔥🔥"

🫠

Meaning: Awkward, embarrassed or can't handle something.

Usage: "He waved at me and i waved at the wrong person 🫠."

🙄

Meaning: Annoyed, unimpressed, or sassy

Usage: "Off course he's late again 🙄."

🤨

Meaning: Side-eye, sus, disbelief

Usage: "You studied 10 hours 🤨."

😩

Meaning: Overwhelmed, exhausted but also used dramatically

Usage: "Bro the wifi is so slow 😩."

Gen Z Memes

"No One:..." Meme

Meaning: Used to describe a random, exaggerated response when no one is prompting it.

Example: "No one: Absolutely no one: Me: *Buys a new plant every week*"

"He's a 10 but..." Meme

Meaning:A rating game where you rate someone's attractiveness and subtract or add points based on red flags.

Example: "He's a 10 but claps when the plane lands."

The Arjun kapoor Meme

Meaning:There's no meaning. it's uncomfortable, aggressive and weirdly serious -- and that's what make it meme-worthy.

Example: "Asked sir to go to washroom in the middle of the class. Le sir *meme plays*"

"SAIRAM...Sairam...sairam" Meme

Meaning:Often used humorously to respond in a dramatic, exaggerated or mock-exasperated tone to something surprising

Example: "Your phone battery dies at 5% mid-game , "*Abba Sai Ram*!!"

"NPC" Memes

Meaning:Referring to people who seem to behave like "non-playable characters"--robotic, predictable, or lacking originality

Example: "A guy walking to work in same outfit everyday = "NPC behavior"

Gen Z Abbreviations

TBH

Meaning: To Be Honest

Usage: "TBH, I don’t know how that happened."

FOMO

Meaning: Fear Of Missing Out

Usage: "I have major FOMO from seeing everyone at that concert."

SMH

Meaning: Shaking My Head

Usage: "SMH, I can’t believe you did that!"

ICYMI

Meaning: In Case You Missed It

Usage: "ICYMI, we’re launching new merch this weekend!"

FR

Meaning: For Real

Usage: "“You’re spitting facts frfr.”"

NGL

Meaning: Not Gonna Lie

Usage: "ngl that song is a banger."

IYKYK

Meaning: If you know you know

Usage: "That ending....iykyk"

LMK

Meaning: Let me know

Usage: "lmk when you're free"

ISTG

Meaning: I swear to god

Usage: "istg if my WiFi cuts again..."

RIZZ

Meaning: charisma/flirting skill

Usage: "I got mad rizz."
